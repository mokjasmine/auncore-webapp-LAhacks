auncore

Go to concerts. Connect with friends. Have fun.

How It Works

auncore is a social networking platform that allows music lovers to achieve the ultimate concert experience.

Discover what concerts your friends are going to and share what concerts you'll be attending by simply connecting your email and Facebook accounts. Using ticket confirmation emails from your inbox, auncore retrieves data about the upcoming concerts you're going to and shares this information with your Facebook friends.
